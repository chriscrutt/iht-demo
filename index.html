Not Just Rewards

Our program goes beyond just rewarding you for good behavior - we also help you identify potential health problems before they become actual problems. With our proactive approach, we aim to keep you healthy and prevent costly medical bills down the line.

Deterministic Response Methodology

Each question is designed to elicit a specific response so health providers can make the appropriate decision on the right course of care.

User-Friendly Interface

Patients are alerted via email or text and can easily respond to questions using their internet connected mobile phone, tablet, or PC. No downloading of an App is needed.

Screenings Tool Based on CAHPS®

Assess and benchmark your patients’ experiences with each practice over a 12-month period, gathering their feedback on quality of provider care and be alerted to potential health issues.

Game Theory Rewards System

A simple game theory reward system is in place that incentivizes participation and creates a consistent positive continued response from patients.
