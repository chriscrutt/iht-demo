Not Just Rewards

Our program goes beyond just rewarding you for good behavior - we also help you identify potential health problems before they become actual problems. With our proactive approach, we aim to keep you healthy and prevent costly medical bills down the line.

Versatile questionnaire

The questionnaire is flexible and can be used to gather information from patients on a wide range of health care issues.

User-Friendly Interface

Patients can easily respond to the questionnaire using their mobile phone, tablet, or PC, making it a user-friendly tool.

Concise and effective questions

The questionnaire includes only two or three Yes/No questions, which makes it easy to complete for patients and quick to analyze for health care professionals.

Follow-up and reward system

After completing the questionnaire, patients can be followed up with by a healthcare professional to discuss their responses and provide guidance. A simple reward system can also be put in place to incentivize participation and make the process more enjoyable.
